How to Use Libre Social Poster

Follow these simple steps to publish your videos on TikTok:

  1. Log in to your Libre Social Poster account.
  2. Upload your video and enter the necessary details.
  3. Set the publishing time and preferences.
  4. Click the "Post" button to publish directly to TikTok.
  5. Monitor your posts via the dashboard.
